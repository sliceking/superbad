Eight Hours.

Its a lot of work

But I feel so satisfied. Working my body like this is really a cure all for me. For the past week and some change my hours have been around 6-7 a day and today I think was the first time I did more than 8. 10 total. I’ve been watching the national team and managers do 12 hour days for the past couple weeks and see how its taken a toll on them. I think I’m capable of doing those hard weeks, back to back, trying to get everyone up to speed. It sounds like fun to me. Right now I’m learning a position called Set, Its like the expo for the front of the house and its a lot of work. A lot more compared to the drive thru or register. Drive thru is like a little separate heaven where the madness doesn’t touch. Sometimes you’ll get weirdos at the window but thats not that bad. The management staff is a bit wild. Theres some good bosses and some anxious ones and some that are probably just plain bad. I wonder what Andrew thinks about how things are going so far. I wish there was time to sit and talk with him but I dont think there is. Culver’s life is busy. Maybe one day he’ll get someone to GM the place for him and then he’ll have time for other things, but from what I’ve seen of him so far I think he’s a good boss.
